1 Fill a small pot with water (about half full)
2 Place the pot on the stove top and set the burner to high
3 When the water comes to a boil add a cup of macaroni noodles to the water
4 When the noodles become tender remove the pot from the heat and drain
5 Wash the drained noodles under cold water
6 Pour the cooked drained noodles into a bowl
7 Roughly chop a few tomatoes into bite-sized chunks and add to the bowl of noodles
8 Roughly chop the peppers, cucumbers, onions, carrots, and black olives. Remember to peel the cucumbers and carrots and quarter the onions
9 Add all of the vegetables to the bowl
10 Sprinkle with your favorite dressing, oil and top with cheese
