1 Pour a little oil into a non-stick frying pan and heat the oil over medium heat on the stove
2 Put a beef patty into the skillet and cook until done
3 Move and flip the patty, do this every couple of minutes using spatula
4 When the burger is cooked throughout, place a slice of cheese on the patty and let it melt
5 Turn off the stove
6 Remove the patty from the pan using a spatula 
7 Place the burger onto the bottom half of a bun
8 Slice a tomato into fairly thin slices 
9 Separate a few pieces of lettuce and cut them in half
10 Put the tomato slices and lettuce slices on top of the burger
11 Garnish with condiments, ketchup, mayo, ranch and mustard
12 Add the remaining bun to the top of the burger
