1 Fill a small pot with water, about half full. Place the pot on the stove top and set the burner to high
2 When the water comes to a boil add a cup of macaroni noodles to the water
3 Roughly chop a few tomatoes into bite-sized chunks and add to the bowl of noodles
4 Roughly chop the peppers, cucumbers, onions, carrots, and black olives. Remember to peel the cucumbers and carrots and quarter the onions. Add all the vegetables to the bowl
5 When the noodles become tender remove the pot from the heat and drain
6 Wash the drained noodles under cold water
7 Pour the cooked drained noodles into the bowl and mix with vegtables
8 Sprinkle with your favorite dressing, oil and top with cheese
