1 Preheat the oven to 400
2 Take the pizza bread from the fridge and let warm on the counter
3 Cut up a hotdog into half inch slices
4 Cut up a green bell pepper into bite-sized chunks
5 Cut up a few mushrooms (enough for a pizza)
6 Again pour some oil into a non-stick frying pan and heat the oil over medium heat
7 Fry the mushrooms slices in the skillet
8 Put enough ketchup on the pizza crust to thinly cover it
9 Place the hot dog/sausage slices, green bell pepper slices, and fried mushrooms on the pizza
10 Add enough shredded mozzarella cheese to cover the pizza
11 Place the pizza in the oven for 20 minutes
