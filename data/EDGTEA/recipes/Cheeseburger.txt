1 Pour a little oil into a non-stick frying pan and heat the oil over medium heat on the stove
2 Put a beef patty into the skillet and cook until done; move and flip the patty every couple of minutes using spatula
3 Slice a tomato and slice an onion to fairly thin slices
4 Separate a few pieces of lettuce and cut them in half
5 When the burger is cooked throughout, place a slice of cheese on the patty and let it melt
6 Remove the patty from the pan using a spatula and place the burger onto the bottom half of a bun
7 Put the tomato slices and lettuce slices on top of the burger
8 Garnish with condiments, ketchup, mayo, ranch and mustard
9 Add the remaining bun to the top of the burger
