2 Add a little milk and salt to taste
3 Beat the egg mixture using a whisk or fork until well blended
4 Pour some oil into a non-stick frying pan and heat the oil over medium heat

6 Pour orange juice into a cup
7 Again pour some oil into a non-stick frying pan and heat the oil over medium heat
8 Fry a piece of bacon in the pan
9 Spread cream cheese onto a bagel half and complete the sandwich with the other half of the bagel
