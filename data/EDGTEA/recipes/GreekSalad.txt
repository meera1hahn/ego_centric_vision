1 Slightly chop a few pieces of lettuce into bite sized chunks
2 Dice a few tomatoes. Do not make them too small, the slices should be large enough to pick up with a fork
3 Peel a cucumber and cut into half slices
4 Cut up an onion and then separate. Keep only pieces large enough to eat easily
5 In a large bowl add the lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and onion slices. Mix the salad together
6 Top with feta cheese and cut up olives and add them to the bowl
7 Sprinkle on vinegar, lemon juice, and olive oil to taste and mix
